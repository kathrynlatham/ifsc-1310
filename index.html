Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

Progressive Enhancement is a form of development that ensures browser compatibility by focusing first on the web content, rather than each browserâ€™s features.

This is important, especially in modern web development, due to the multitude of operating systems and browsers that exist in the world today. It is impossible to guess how your end-user will be accessing your site, so giving them the best experience possible requires a design that is independent of their unique web browsing choices.
